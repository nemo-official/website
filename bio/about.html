My Coding Journey

My love for coding started at around 13, I kind of knew what coding is. I knew what it can be used for, the different types of languages and soon but I knew absolutely nothing on how to apply it so I kind of put it on hold. Few years went by, learned different skills, I grew an interest in physics, there was a huge library next to our house that had all kinds of books, spent most of my early teenage life there (yes, I was that kind of kid who spent all their free time in a library). At around 16 I decided to resume my coding interest, started with web dev then moved to game dev using unity. After that, I tried a bit of app testing and graphic design. At 16 I got my first internship at a Lebanese Start-up called “Carpolo”. I was supposed to be a graphic designer and get paid but the sponsors who sponsored them had a rule that the intern had to be a university student. So I ended up as an “unpaid” intern (I know, it sucks). So I was more of an app tester than a graphic designer. After I was done I worked on different things, such Cryptocurrencies. Did some mining and trading for a few months. Now I’m back to full-stack web dev.

My Life In A Nutshell

As you already might know, my name is Hani Janzi. I was born in Sweden on August11th 00. I moved to Lebanon when I was around 11 months. Lived most of my life in Lebanon constantly changing schools. But at the age of 13 my family and I moved back to Sweden (LOL yeah I know we move a lot) went to over 4 or 5 different schools in 2 years, after that we moved back to Lebanon (yup… more moving) finished my last 3 years of school in Lebanon. Now I’m doing my freshman year at BAU (Beirut Arab University). Planning on studying physics in the future, hoping to get a masters and PhD.
